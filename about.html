DeShaun L. Gordon-King has been noted for his imaginative and effective programming. Through careful selection of both widely and lesser known repertoire with a focus on composers of African and Afro-Latinx backgrounds and unifying themes, DeShaun creates musical experiences rather than merely concerts.
